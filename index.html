Example topics (Baltimore / Maryland)
Good questions Civic Lab can answer
  • What is the “done definition” for a specific capital project, and what’s blocking delivery?
  • Where are service gaps geographically (and what’s the cheapest fix)?
  • Which programs show measurable outcomes vs cost?
  • Procurement scan: who’s doing this well elsewhere, and what would it cost here?
  • Policy options: 3–5 paths, tradeoffs, risks, and what data would settle the debate.
