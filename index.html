Citizen-led Ground Support for Public Problem-solving.

Civic Lab mobilizes citizen researchers to deliver decision-ready briefs and analysis—fast. We provide ground support to overloaded teams working on housing, transit, and public wealth in the Baltimore–DC region.

We turn complex civic challenges into usable artifacts—briefs, analysis, and reports—that busy teams can plug into meetings, hearings, and campaigns.
