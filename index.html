Good partner questions

  • What decision are we trying to make, and what are the realistic options?
  • What does success look like, and what should we measure?
  • What’s blocking delivery, and what are the leverage points?
  • Where are the gaps (by neighborhood/region), and what’s the lowest-cost fix?
  • Who’s doing this well elsewhere, and what would it cost here?
